Description

Our brand new survival biscuit, Hardtack™, is the new best way to keep yourself alive during long hikes, expeditions, and nuclear wars. It's cheap, long lasting, tasty, healthy, and its full of energy to keep you going throughout the day! The food bar, hence the name, is based of hardtack, an army ration during WWI and WWII. Hardtack was make of flour, water, and sometimes salt, then cooked mulitple times to remove moisture and make it last longer. Like hardtack, our product can also be soaked in soup or other liquids to enhance flavour, and it lasts for weeks!

ABOUT

We designed our food bar to serve as your only food source for a day, 6 or 7 giving the daily energy requirement for grown adults, while one bar can serve as a quick energy boost for multiple people due to its size. The ingredients included in one batch, which makes 6 bars, is as follows:
1 cup of whole-wheat flour, 1/2 cup of all purpose flour, 1/2 cup of rolled oats, 1 tablespoon of sugar, 1 teaspoon of sodium bicarbonate, 1/2 cup of powdered milk, 1/2 cup of water, and 25 grams of butter.

LOCATION

All of our food bars are handmade in the kitchens of the Sydney Secondary College Balmain Campus, which are cleaned after every single batch. We use the most eco-friendly ingredients available and we enslave employ many students to cook the food bars.
